# 💳 Credit Card Financial Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project features a comprehensive Power BI dashboard that analyzes credit card transactions and customer data. It provides stakeholders with actionable insights into revenue generation, customer demographics, transaction behaviors, and financial performance across different card categories.

## 🎯 Objective
The primary goal of this dashboard is to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) related to credit card usage and to segment customers based on their financial behavior, income, and demographics. This enables data-driven decision-making for marketing strategies, product development, and risk management.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **Data Visualization:** Power BI
* **Data Processing:** Power Query, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* **Design:** Custom layouts and interactive visual elements

## 📊 Key Insights & Findings

### Executive Summary & KPIs
* **Total Revenue:** The total revenue generated across the portfolio is **$55M**.
* **Transaction Volume:** There were **656K** total transactions, amounting to a total transaction value of **$45M**.
* **Interest Earned:** The total interest earned stands at **$8M**.
* **Customer Base:** Total customer income base is **$576M** with a slightly higher revenue contribution from male customers (**$30M**) compared to female customers (**$25M**).

### 👥 Customer Demographics & Profiling
* **Age & Marital Status:** The **40-50 age group** is the primary driver of revenue. Additionally, **married** customers contribute more heavily to the total revenue than single customers.
* **Income Divide:** High-income earners account for the largest revenue share ($29M total), heavily skewed by male customers ($22M Male vs. $7M Female). Conversely, the low-income segment's revenue is driven almost entirely by female customers.
* **Occupation & Education:** **Businessmen** and **White-collar** professionals are the most profitable occupational groups. Educationally, **Graduates** represent the highest revenue-generating demographic.

### 💳 Card Usage & Transaction Patterns
* **Card Dominance:** The **Blue card** is overwhelmingly the most popular and profitable tier, accounting for roughly **83%** of the total revenue (~$46M out of $55M), dwarfing the Silver, Gold, and Platinum categories.
* **Transaction Method:** Customers highly prefer traditional physical transactions; the **Swipe** method generates the vast majority of revenue, followed by Chip, with Online transactions trailing significantly.
* **Top Expenditure Categories:** The highest volume of customer spending goes toward **Bills**, followed by **Entertainment**, **Fuel**, and **Groceries**. 

### 📅 Temporal Trends
* **Quarterly Performance:** Revenue and transaction volumes show steady growth throughout the year, peaking in **Q3** (generating over $14M in revenue and 166.6K in transaction volume) before experiencing a slight dip in Q4.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Clone the repository to your local machine.
2. Ensure you have **Microsoft Power BI Desktop** installed.
3. Open the `.pbix` file included in this repository.
4. Interact with the slicers (e.g., Week_Start_Date, Card Category filters) to explore the data dynamically.
